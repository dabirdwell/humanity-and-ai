## Why It Cascades

When you invest in Safety, people can sleep. When people can sleep, their mental health improves. When mental health improves, learning capacity increases. When learning capacity increases, employment follows. When employment follows, the social contract begins to rebuild.

The cascade isn't a theory — it's how human needs actually work. Housing stability predicts school performance. Food security predicts health outcomes. Mental health predicts everything. These connections are well-documented in decades of public health research. What's less common is designing policy that accounts for them as a system rather than treating each need as a separate line item.

That's what Foundation does. The 16 components aren't a wish list. They're an interdependent infrastructure, and the ripple effect is the evidence.

Try starting with UBI — the economic floor. Watch how removing survival anxiety cascades upward through housing, food, healthcare, and ultimately the social contract. Then try starting with Education. Different entry point, different cascade, same destination: a population with the bandwidth to build.
