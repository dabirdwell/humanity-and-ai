## What Is Clarity?

Clarity is a reading companion. Not a summarizer — those already exist, and they teach you to skip the work that builds understanding. Clarity sits alongside what you're reading and helps you see what's actually happening in the text.

It asks questions you didn't think to ask. It surfaces connections between what you're reading now and what you read last month. It notices when you're skimming past something important and pulls you back. It adapts to your reading level without condescending.

## How It Works

Feed Clarity anything — an article, a research paper, a chapter, a legal document. It builds a comprehension map in real time: what you understood, what you glossed over, what matters for your goals. The AI doesn't replace your reading. It deepens it.

The teaching layer draws from cognitive science research on how comprehension actually works — not speed-reading tricks, but the structural understanding that lets you engage with complex material and retain it.

## Why It Exists

We read more than any generation in history and understand less of it. Clarity is the first Brain Mastery app because reading comprehension is the foundation everything else builds on — critical thinking, civic engagement, professional development, creative work. If you can't read deeply, you can't think deeply.
