## What Is The Inference?

The Inference is a policy intelligence briefing series that tracks AI legislation, executive orders, and regulatory developments relevant to Oklahoma. Each issue connects federal AI policy to state-level impact — translating dense policy language into actionable analysis for decision-makers, journalists, and engaged citizens.

## Key Features

- **Issue-based briefings** — Each Inference issue covers a specific policy development with context, analysis, and implications for Oklahoma.
- **State-federal connection** — National AI policy doesn't exist in a vacuum. The Inference maps how federal frameworks, executive orders, and proposed legislation intersect with Oklahoma's own regulatory landscape.
- **Plain language analysis** — Policy documents are dense by design. The Inference makes them accessible without dumbing them down.

## Why It Exists

AI policy is moving fast, and most of the coverage is either too technical for general audiences or too shallow for decision-makers. The Inference fills the gap — rigorous analysis written for people who need to act on it.
